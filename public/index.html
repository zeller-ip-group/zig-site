About Us

Zeller IP Group specializes in intellectual property protection and enforcement, including all aspects of patents, trademarks and copyrights. Like you, we’re entrepreneurs, and we’re uniquely positioned to help you align your intellectual property strategy with your business goals.

Practice Areas

Patent Drafting & Prosecution

Our patent practitioners handle the initial preparation and filing of patent applications and the subsequent prosecution of the applications before the USPTO and foreign patent offices.

IP & Technology Transactions

We help our clients maximize the value of their most precious assets by combining our technical and legal expertise with a willingness to dive deeper into our client’s technology.

Trademarks & Copyrights

Our practitioners provide a full range of services relating to trademark, copyright, trade dress, and domain name matters, including strategic counseling and prosecution work.

Kyle Zeller is the founder and Managing Shareholder of Zeller IP Group.   • Contract due diligence, including review of intellectual property licensing, research and development agreements, software and content development agreements and other intellectual property related agreements and communication of findings to clients through written and oral communications.

Erik Dykema, Counsel

Erik Dykema is Counsel for Zeller IP Group. Mr. Dykema’s primary practice focuses on patent litigation and trademark matters. Serge Krimnus, Counsel

Serge Krimnus is a patent attorney with over five years’ experience as a software engineer, bringing substantial technical knowledge and experience to his practice. Serge has represented clients in U.S. district court in patent and trademark infringement cases, prosecuted patent and trademark applications, and represented clients in domain name arbitrations. Serge’s experience in preparing and prosecuting patent applications and litigating patents spans a variety of technologies from computer software and hardware to mechanical engineering and medical devices. Serge also counsels clients on issues relating to selection, registration and protection of trademarks, and has experience in opposition and cancellation proceedings before the Trademark Trial and Appeal Board.

Serge is an Adjunct Professor of Law and Adjunct Professor for Clinical Law at Brooklyn Law School, where he teaches Patent Prosecution and supervises law students at the Brooklyn Law Incubator and Policy Clinic, which counsels startup technology companies on patent and trademark issues. He has also been a guest lecturer on design patent law at New York University.
